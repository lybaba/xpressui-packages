These presets demonstrate the absolute flexibility of the form without writing a single line of CSS.

## 💡 How to test the background image?
To test the `Panel Focus` and `Full Bleed` styles, go to the **Media** menu in your WordPress, upload a beautiful image (e.g., an abstract Unsplash background), click on the image, and copy the "**File URL**" to paste it into the *Background Image URL* field.
